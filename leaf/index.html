Your Kindle highlights, alive on your desktop.

Leaf turns your Kindle clippings into a searchable local library and a rotating desktop wallpaper, so the best lines from your books stay close without interrupting your work.

Join Public Beta Watch Demo Video

Available for macOS. Built native in Swift.

Simple enough to live in the background. Useful enough to keep open.

Leaf is built around the parts of reading that are easy to lose: the quotes, the books, and the small reminders worth seeing again.

1

Import Kindle clippings

Drop in your My Clippings.txt file and Leaf builds a local quote library from your highlights, books, and authors.

2

Curate what appears

Enable or disable books, search across quotes, and keep the wallpaper focused on the reading you actually want to revisit.

3

Rotate wallpapers

Choose background images and let Leaf render quiet quote wallpapers that change over time on your Mac desktop.

A reading library that stays personal.

Browse imported books, see highlight counts, and decide which titles are allowed into rotation. Leaf is designed for a real personal library, not a one-off quote generator.

Search highlights before they become wallpaper.

Find quotes by text, book, or author. Sort recent highlights, filter the library, and keep manual additions separate from Kindle imports.

Bring your own backgrounds.

Leaf lets you manage the images behind your quotes, from calm paintings to photos to your own abstract wallpapers. The selected purple wallpaper is included in the beta experience.

Privacy Policy

Leaf is designed as a local-first Mac app. Your imported Kindle highlights, books, authors, background choices, and generated wallpapers are stored on your Mac.

The beta does not sell personal data or use advertising trackers. If you email feedback or report a bug, the information you choose to send is used to respond and improve Leaf.

Because Leaf is in public beta, diagnostics may change as the app evolves. Any future data collection should be documented here before release.

Terms of Use

Leaf is provided as beta software. It may contain bugs, change behavior, or be unavailable while the app is being improved.

You are responsible for your own Kindle files, highlights, screenshots, backgrounds, and backups. Leaf is provided without warranties and should not be relied on for preserving important data.

By using the beta, you agree to use Leaf lawfully and to share feedback, bug reports, and feature requests respectfully.
